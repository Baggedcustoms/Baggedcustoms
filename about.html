About BaggedCustoms

Founded by a longtime GTA V modder, BaggedCustoms is your go-to source for high-quality, custom vehicle mods, especially focused on slammed trucks, real-life builds, and unique themed creations. With over 600 active patrons and a community built over years, BaggedCustoms has grown into a respected name in the modding scene.

Our mods are built from the ground up, with careful attention to realism and style. Our creations are used globally by YouTubers, FiveM servers, and solo players who want top-tier quality in their rides.

This site serves as a curated catalog of our work, providing a clean, filterable visual index of our entire mod archive.
