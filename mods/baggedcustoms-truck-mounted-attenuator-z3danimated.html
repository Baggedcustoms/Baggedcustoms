BaggedCustoms Truck Mounted Attenuator [Z3D][Animated]

### DO NOT MANUALLY MOVE ANY PART OF THE BED###
If you move the bed parts you will break the animation. Use the meta files from the BCF750TMA for all the animation and lights to function. Simple merge your new truck body into this mod to reatin all functions. Simply adjust your truck to fit the bed unless you know how to reconfigure the animations.
